About me

Hello! I'm Tamires Lelis, a UX/UI Designer currently working as UX Designer Consultant. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering and have been working as a UX Designer since 2017.

Human behavior has always fascinated me and that is the reason why I enjoy creating user-centric and delightful systems or Apps. As a UX Designer, I am constantly searching for better practices and also experimenting new things. Working and collaborating with teams is something I am very passionate about. During my spare time, I like to share my knowledge with my friends so that they can learn new things and remove any doubts they may have.

My hobbies include traveling, talking with people, watching movies and listening good music.

If you need any assistance or would like to know more about my work, please feel free to contact me.

You can also follow me on LinkedIn, or reach out via email.
