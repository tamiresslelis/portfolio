WhatApp Redesign

Challenge and Goal

  1. Bring some hypotheses about the existing user problems in WhatsApp
  2. Write a guide for an in-depth interview for your hypotheses validation.
  3. Create a prototype to validate your solution

My Design Process

Understand

Fist step I used the Matrix, CSD (Certainty, Supposition and Doubts) because this method give me insight where I should focus and focus efforts on the project . It works from one of the top priorities:

  1. What we already know about the project?
  2. What are our hypotheses or what did we supposed to know about the project?
  3. What questions to ask?

I suppose 3 hypotheses, I realized in-depth interview people who are WhatsApp users for validated or unvalidated these hypothesis

Why in-depth interview?

Because it helps to better understand the goals, complains and the user needs, offering a chance to get a direct feedback.

I had driven the in-depth interview in the half structured format where I had followed a script with predefined questions, but I was free to ask open questions if I was interested doing it during the interview. Predefined questions are a guideline, but it doesn’t need to be made in an exactly order the way they were defined.

It's like funnel, I asked some broad questions , then I will be directioning it to my goals.

User Group Identification

  1. Observations or ideas from a research
  2. Ideas that are on the surface in design-ideation meetings
  3. Ideas about UX strategy and vision
